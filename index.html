Creating Lasting Change

We work in over 25 countries around the world, focusing on emergency response, education, healthcare, and economic development to create lasting change.

Our Mission & Vision

Learn about what drives us and the change we want to see in the world

Who We Are

HopeForWorld is an international nonprofit organization dedicated to creating lasting change in the lives of children, families, and communities living in poverty and injustice around the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to follow our example of compassion by serving those in need through sustainable solutions that promote dignity, self-reliance, and community resilience.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every person lives in a safe, sustainable community where their basic needs are met, their human dignity is respected, and their potential is nurtured.

Our Approach

We believe in community-led development. Rather than imposing solutions, we work with communities to identify their needs and develop sustainable approaches that create lasting change.

About Us

Get to know the organization behind the mission

Our Story

Founded in 1998, HopeForWorld began as a small group of volunteers responding to natural disasters. Over the years, we've grown into an international organization with programs in over 25 countries, but our commitment to grassroots solutions and community partnership has never changed.

Our Values

Compassion: We approach our work with empathy and respect for the communities we serve.

Integrity: We are transparent and accountable in all our actions and use of resources.

Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in all our programs and operations.

Partnership: We work alongside communities, respecting their knowledge and capacity.

Innovation: We continuously seek new and better solutions to complex problems.
